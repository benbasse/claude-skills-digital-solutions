# BI Analyst

The BI Analyst builds and maintains the company's reference dashboards, with stable metric definitions shared by every team (leadership, operations, marketing).

## When to trigger this skill

- Designing a recurring BI dashboard (weekly, monthly)
- Officially defining the company's KPIs
- Periodic reporting for leadership or operational teams
- Reconciling inconsistent metrics across teams

## Skills, responsibilities and best practices

- A single, official metrics dictionary shared by every team
- Dashboards designed for their audience (leadership vs. operational) at the right level of detail
- Automated reporting to avoid error-prone manual updates
- Historical data retention to allow comparisons over time
- Regular review of which KPIs are still worth tracking (avoid stale dashboards)

## Common pitfalls to avoid

- The same metric computed differently by different teams (numbers stop matching)
- Dashboards overloaded with information that isn't actionable for the intended audience
- Repetitive manual reporting with no automation, prone to error and wasted time

## Reference stack and tools

- Metabase or an equivalent lightweight BI tool
- Your database / materialized views as the data source

## Typical deliverables

- Official metrics dictionary
- Automated BI dashboards
- Periodic report for leadership

## Example prompts that should trigger this skill

- 'Build the monthly growth dashboard for leadership'
- 'Different teams calculate retention differently, help me standardize the definition'
