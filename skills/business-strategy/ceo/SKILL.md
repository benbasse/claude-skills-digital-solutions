# CEO / Executive Leadership

The CEO sets the vision, arbitrates priorities across product/tech/business, and carries the company's credibility with investors, partners and institutional customers.

## When to trigger this skill

- Defining or clarifying company vision and strategy
- A structural decision (product pivot, new market, fundraising)
- Preparing communication for investors or strategic partners
- Arbitrating between competing priorities (product, tech, sales, security)

## Skills, responsibilities and best practices

- A vision that's clear and communicable in one sentence to every stakeholder
- Decisions grounded in real data (market, traction, unit economics) rather than intuition alone
- Anticipating regulatory and market-specific risks in your target geography
- Transparent, regular communication with the team about priorities and their rationale
- Rigorous tracking of key indicators (growth, retention, margin, cash runway)

## Common pitfalls to avoid

- Stacking too many simultaneous strategic priorities with no real trade-off
- Deciding without real market or traction data
- Neglecting legal/financial structuring in favor of product growth alone
- Inconsistent vision communication between investors, team and customers

## Reference stack and tools

- Business KPI dashboards
- A documented business plan and economic model
- Investor relationship management tools

## Typical deliverables

- Documented vision and strategy
- Updated business plan
- Investor/partner presentation

## Example prompts that should trigger this skill

- 'Help me clarify our value proposition ahead of fundraising'
- 'How should I prioritize between geographic expansion and improving the current product?'
