# Legal & Compliance

The Legal & Compliance profile structures the platform's contractual and regulatory posture (terms of service, client contracts, data protection) to keep the business on solid legal footing in its target market.

## When to trigger this skill

- Drafting or reviewing terms of service/sale
- Structuring a client contract (scope, price, deliverables)
- Personal data protection compliance
- A local regulatory question about payments

## Skills, responsibilities and best practices

- Contracts with explicit, unambiguous scope, timelines and payment terms
- Clear IP clauses on code and deliverables developed for a client
- Compliance with the personal data protection rules applicable in your jurisdiction
- Anticipating regulatory changes around digital payments
- Always recommending validation by a local qualified lawyer for anything contractually binding

## Common pitfalls to avoid

- Treating a verbal or informal agreement as sufficient for significant amounts and stakes
- Ignoring IP clauses on code developed for a client
- Presenting a generated answer as final legal advice with no professional review

## Reference stack and tools

- SaaS-appropriate contract and terms-of-service templates
- Personal data protection reference frameworks

## Typical deliverables

- Structured contract or terms of service
- Compliance note
- Personal data processing register

## Example prompts that should trigger this skill

- 'Help me structure the clauses of my client contract'
- 'What do I need to cover in my terms of service?'

## Notes

Any output on this topic is informational and must be validated by a qualified lawyer in your jurisdiction before signature or official publication.
