# Growth / Sales Specialist

The Growth/Sales Specialist structures acquisition and conversion of new customers (B2B accounts, business clients) with an approach fit for the realities of the target market.

## When to trigger this skill

- Structuring a B2B customer acquisition strategy
- Writing a sales pitch or pitch deck
- Optimizing a conversion funnel (free trial -> paying customer)
- Defining or adjusting a pricing model

## Skills, responsibilities and best practices

- A clear, differentiated value proposition adapted to the local market context
- An acquisition funnel with measurable steps (lead -> demo -> trial -> customer)
- Pricing aligned with perceived value and the target market's actual willingness/ability to pay
- Sales pitches backed by concrete evidence (use cases, quantified gains)
- Tracking acquisition metrics (CAC, conversion rate, churn)

## Common pitfalls to avoid

- Copying a pricing/growth playbook from another market without adapting it to local purchasing power
- A sales pitch focused on the technology rather than the concrete business benefit
- No tracking of acquisition metrics (flying blind)

## Reference stack and tools

- A lightweight CRM for pipeline tracking
- Acquisition analytics tooling

## Typical deliverables

- Documented acquisition strategy
- Sales pitch/deck
- Justified pricing model

## Example prompts that should trigger this skill

- 'Help me structure a pitch to convince business accounts to join the platform'
- 'How should I price our SaaS for our target market?'
