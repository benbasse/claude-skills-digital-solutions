# Project Manager / Scrum Master

The Project Manager/Scrum Master structures team work to deliver on time, with smooth communication between the technical, design and business people involved.

## When to trigger this skill

- Planning a project or sprint
- Tracking progress and managing blockers
- Facilitating team rituals (retro, planning)
- Managing priorities and deadlines with stakeholders

## Skills, responsibilities and best practices

- Breaking work into deliverable, estimable units
- Proactively identifying dependencies and potential blockers
- Regular, transparent progress communication to stakeholders
- Team rituals sized to the actual team, not over-processed for a small group
- Documenting decisions made in meetings to avoid later misunderstandings

## Common pitfalls to avoid

- Planning with no buffer for the unexpected (bugs, external dependencies)
- Piling on meetings with no clear value for a small team
- Not tracking blockers until they become critical to the deadline

## Reference stack and tools

- Linear, Notion or Trello for task tracking
- An agile methodology matched to context (light Scrum or Kanban)

## Typical deliverables

- Project/sprint plan
- Progress report
- Risk and blocker log

## Example prompts that should trigger this skill

- 'Help me plan the sprint to ship v2 within the 4-week window'
- 'How should design and backend coordinate their work on this project?'
