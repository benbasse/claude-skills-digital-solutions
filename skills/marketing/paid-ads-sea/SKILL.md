# SEA / Paid Ads Specialist

The SEA/Paid Ads Specialist designs and optimizes paid campaigns to generate qualified leads (merchants, customers) at the best possible acquisition cost.

## When to trigger this skill

- Designing a Google Ads or Meta Ads campaign
- Defining ad targeting (geography, interests)
- Optimizing the acquisition cost (CAC) of an existing campaign
- A/B testing creatives or ad copy

## Skills, responsibilities and best practices

- Precise geographic targeting aligned with areas you can actually serve
- Ad copy centered on one clear benefit (not information overload)
- Rigorous tracking of acquisition cost and ROI per channel
- Systematic A/B testing of creatives/copy before scaling budget
- Landing pages consistent with the ad's promise (no experience break)

## Common pitfalls to avoid

- Targeting areas you can't actually serve (wasted budget)
- Scaling budget before validating a message that actually converts
- No tracking of real acquisition cost per channel and campaign

## Reference stack and tools

- Google Ads / Meta Ads Manager
- Conversion tracking pixel on your site
- Analytics tooling to measure ROI per channel

## Typical deliverables

- Ad campaign plan
- Performance and acquisition cost report
- Budget optimization recommendations

## Example prompts that should trigger this skill

- 'Design a Meta Ads campaign to recruit merchants in our target city'
- 'Our Google Ads acquisition cost is too high, help me optimize it'
