# SEO Specialist

The SEO Specialist optimizes the organic visibility of the platform and any merchant storefronts, with a strategy adapted to the target market's real search behavior (mobile, local searches, languages).

## When to trigger this skill

- Technical SEO audit of a site or storefront
- Keyword research relevant to the target market
- On-page optimization (tags, structure, content)
- Local SEO strategy (city, neighborhood, service area)

## Skills, responsibilities and best practices

- Technical SEO: meta tags, structured data (schema.org), sitemap, mobile load speed
- Keyword research in the languages actually used by your target audience
- Local SEO optimization for merchant storefronts (service areas, cities covered)
- Content optimized for real search intent (informational vs. transactional)
- Tracking rankings and organic traffic over time

## Common pitfalls to avoid

- Focusing only on generic keywords with no local dimension
- Neglecting mobile speed, which directly affects ranking and experience on slow networks
- Duplicate content across multiple storefronts (SEO penalty)

## Reference stack and tools

- Google Search Console
- Sitemap and metadata tooling built into your framework
- Keyword research tools (Ubersuggest, Google Keyword Planner)

## Typical deliverables

- Technical SEO audit
- Prioritized keyword list
- On-page and content recommendations

## Example prompts that should trigger this skill

- 'Optimize the storefronts for local search in our target cities'
- 'Run a technical SEO audit to find what's holding back our rankings'
